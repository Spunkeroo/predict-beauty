The gel formula applies smoothly, cures in 60 seconds under the included lamp, and lasts 2-3 weeks without chipping. For $22, you get what would cost $300+ in salon visits.

How to apply gel nails at home:

  1. Push back cuticles and shape nails with a file (never cut cuticles)
  2. Lightly buff nail surface for adhesion
  3. Apply thin base coat, cure 60 seconds under UV lamp
  4. Apply first thin color coat, cure 60 seconds
  5. Apply second color coat, cure 60 seconds
  6. Apply top coat, cure 60 seconds
  7. Wipe tacky layer with rubbing alcohol and cotton pad

Weekly Nail Care Routine

  • Daily: Apply cuticle oil morning and night. This is the single most impactful thing you can do for nail health
  • Weekly: Shape nails with a glass file (never metal). File in one direction only to prevent splitting
  • Bi-weekly: Gently push back cuticles after a shower when they are soft. Never cut cuticles
  • Monthly: Soak nails in warm olive oil for 10 minutes to deeply hydrate. Follow with cuticle oil and hand cream
  • Always: Wear gloves when cleaning with chemicals. Water and detergents are the #1 cause of weak, peeling nails

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does gel nail polish last?

Properly applied gel polish lasts 2-3 weeks without chipping. Professional salon gel typically lasts slightly longer (3-4 weeks) due to precise application and stronger lamps. At-home gel kits with quality brands like Beetles perform nearly as well.

Is gel polish bad for your nails?

Gel polish itself does not damage nails. Damage comes from improper removal — peeling or picking gel off tears layers of your natural nail. Always soak off gel with acetone and foil wraps. Between gel applications, use a nail strengthener like OPI Nail Envy.

How do I make nail polish last longer?

Apply thin coats (thick coats peel), wrap the tip of each nail with polish, use a quality base and top coat, apply cuticle oil daily (hydrated nails hold polish better), and wear gloves when cleaning.

What is the best nail strengthener?

OPI Nail Envy is the #1 rated nail strengthener by both professionals and consumers. It contains wheat protein and calcium to reinforce nail structure. Apply every other day for 2 weeks, then weekly for maintenance.

How often should I apply cuticle oil?

Apply cuticle oil at least twice daily — morning and before bed. For best results, apply after every hand wash. Consistent cuticle oil use is the #1 thing you can do for healthier, stronger nails.
